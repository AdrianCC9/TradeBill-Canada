# Terms and Disclaimer Draft

This draft should be reviewed before launch.

## No Professional Advice

TradeBill Canada is a document creation tool. It does not provide accounting, bookkeeping, tax, legal, or financial advice.

## User Responsibility

Users are responsible for:

- Confirming applicable tax requirements.
- Confirming registration obligations.
- Reviewing invoice and estimate details before sending.
- Keeping their own records.
- Backing up important exported PDFs.

## Tax Disclaimer

```text
Tax calculations are provided for convenience only. Users are responsible for confirming applicable tax requirements.
```

## Local Data

Version 1 stores app data locally on the user's device. The user is responsible for managing their device, backups, and exported documents.

## Purchases

Lifetime unlock is a one-time in-app purchase handled by Apple. Restore purchase should be available in the app.

## Limitation

The app should not guarantee that a generated estimate or invoice complies with every tax, legal, industry, or client-specific requirement.
