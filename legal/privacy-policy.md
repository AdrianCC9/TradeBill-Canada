# Privacy Policy Draft

Effective date: To be added before launch.

TradeBill Canada is designed as a local-only invoice and estimate app. This draft should be reviewed before App Store submission.

## Information Stored by the App

TradeBill Canada may store the following information locally on your device:

- Business profile details.
- Client names and contact details.
- Estimate and invoice details.
- Line items.
- Notes and terms.
- Logo image.
- Signature image.
- App settings.
- Purchase unlock state.

## Local Storage

TradeBill Canada does not require an account and does not use a backend server for version 1. Your business, client, and document data is stored locally on your device.

If you delete the app, local app data may be deleted according to iOS behavior.

## Sharing PDFs

When you choose to share or export a PDF, the PDF may be sent through Apple sharing tools or third-party apps you select. Those services are governed by their own privacy policies.

## Purchases

TradeBill Canada uses Apple's in-app purchase system for the lifetime unlock. Purchase processing is handled by Apple. The app may store a local record that lifetime access has been unlocked.

## Analytics

Version 1 should not include third-party analytics unless this policy is updated before release.

## Data Collection

Version 1 should not collect personal data on a developer-controlled server.
