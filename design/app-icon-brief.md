# App Icon Brief

## Goal

Create an icon that feels practical, clean, Canadian, and trustworthy without looking like accounting software.

## Direction

Primary concept:

```text
A clean invoice/document shape with a turquoise checkmark or accent line, using white and turquoise.
```

Avoid:

- Dollar signs as the main symbol.
- Maple leaf cliches unless very subtle.
- Busy receipt details.
- Gradients that feel like fintech crypto apps.
- Tiny text.

## Palette

Use:

- Turquoise `#18BFC3`.
- Deep Turquoise `#0E8F94`.
- White `#FFFFFF`.
- Dark Text `#172326` only if needed.

## Shape Ideas

Option A:

- Turquoise rounded-square background.
- White document outline.
- Deep turquoise folded corner.
- Small checkmark or horizontal invoice lines.

Option B:

- White rounded-square background.
- Turquoise document.
- Deep turquoise checkmark.
- Subtle border.

Option C:

- Turquoise background.
- White monogram `TB`.
- Small document line motif.

## Figma Checklist

- Design at 1024 x 1024.
- Keep important shapes inside Apple's safe visual area.
- Test at small sizes.
- Export required App Store and Xcode asset sizes from the final source.
- Do not include transparent background for final App Store icon.

## Splash / Launch Screen

Use a simple native launch screen:

- White background.
- Centered app mark only.
- No loading text.
- No marketing copy.
